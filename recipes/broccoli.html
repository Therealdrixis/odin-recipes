Broccoli

Description

Air fried broccoli is quite a tasty treat! It can be made in a number of different ways, however, we will be using the air fryer.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash and trim broccoli to bite size pieces.
  2. In a bowl, toss the broccoli with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  3. Evenly spread out the broccoli into your air fryer (Do not over crowd the broccoli)
  4. Set the air fryer to 390 degrees and cook it for 8 minutes.
