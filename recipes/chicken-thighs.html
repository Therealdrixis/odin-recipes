Chicken Thighs

Description

Chicken thighs are high in fat and therefore high in flavor. They also reheat way better than breasts. Make sure to trim the fat to your liking.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Trim the fat from the chicken thighs.
  2. Place the thighs into a bowl and mix it up with olive oil and salt.
  3. Place the seasoned thighs into the air fryer and spread them out.
  4. Set the air fryer to 390 degrees for 12 minutes.
