Rice

Description

Rice is a very basic dish but we can make it in the all in one air fryer unit using the pressure cooker portion.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken stock
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Rinse your rice in a strainer. It's done when the water runs clear.
  2. Put the washed rice into the pot with equal parts chicken stock.
  3. Set the unti to pressure cook and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Slow release the steam when it finishes.
